H.R. 4978 - Online Privacy Act of 2019

Authors: Eshoo, Anna G. and Lofgren, Zoe

Published: December 2019

URL: https://www.congress.gov/bill/116th-congress/house-bill/4978/text

Tags: 2019 Bill Data Protection Online Privacy Act of 2019 Privacy Rights US Congress

Abstract: To provide for individual rights relating to privacy of personal information, to establish privacy and security requirements for covered entities relating to personal information, and to establish an agency to be known as the United States Digital Privacy Agency to enforce such rights and requirements, and for other purposes.
