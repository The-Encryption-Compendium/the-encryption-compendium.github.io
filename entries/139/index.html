OECD Guidelines for Cryptography Policy - OECD

Authors: OECD

Published: March 1997

URL: https://www.oecd.org/sti/ieconomy/guidelinesforcryptographypolicy.htm

Abstract: Data and research on e-commerce including measuring the information economy, internet economy outlook, open internet, openness, key ICT indicators, digital economy policy papers., The Guidelines are primarily aimed at governments, in terms of the policy recommendations herein, but with anticipation that they will be widely read and followed by both the private and public sectors.
